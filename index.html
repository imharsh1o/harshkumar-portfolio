Product Thinking

Key Decisions Made

Rejected
Rejected Apify — paid scraping tool considered for Wellfound/Cutshort. Cost unjustified at proof-of-concept stage. Principle: validate before spending.
Pivoted
Cutshort → Naukri → Remotive — Make.com HTTP module blocked by Cutshort and Naukri APIs. Moved to Remotive — the only free, accessible, Make-compatible job API.
Pivoted
LinkedIn → job board APIs — LinkedIn scraping carries legal and technical risk. Pivoted to job boards with accessible data. Faster, safer, equally effective.
Focused
2 sources over 4 — Prioritised 2 pipeline sources working end-to-end over 4 partial integrations. Shipped a working v1 and proved value before expanding.
Focused
Role filters added — Added category filters (software-dev, devops, backend, full-stack) to improve lead relevance and reduce noise in the sheet.
